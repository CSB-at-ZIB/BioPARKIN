def contrib::singletonmixin::singletonmixin_Public_TestCase::testTryToInstantiateWithoutGetInstance (   self)

Demonstrates that singletons can ONLY be instantiated through getInstance, as long as they call Singleton.__init__ during construction.

If this check is not required, you don't need to call Singleton.__init__().
